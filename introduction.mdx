## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Profile">
    Register and set up your user profile with travel interests and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Your Trip">
    Specify your destination, dates, and the type of experience you're looking for
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Generates Itinerary">
    Google Gemini analyzes your interests and generates a personalized day-by-day itinerary
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore & Customize">
    Review your trip plan, explore points of interest, and make adjustments as needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology Stack

MayTravel is built with modern technologies to deliver a fast, reliable, and intelligent travel planning experience:

- **Frontend**: React for a responsive user interface
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express for robust API services
- **AI Engine**: Google Gemini Model Language with RAG for intelligent planning
- **Geographic Data**: Google Maps Platform for location services
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for flexible, relational data management
